VCPulse - Generate PDF

Generate a PDF version of the Weekly Intelligence Brief

Quick Steps to Generate PDF:

  1. Click the button below to open the Weekly Brief
  2. Press Cmd+P (Mac) or Ctrl+P (Windows/Linux)
  3. In the print dialog:
    • Destination: Save as PDF
    • Layout: Portrait
    • Paper size: A4 or Letter
    • Margins: None
    • Options: ✓ Background graphics (Important!)
  4. Click "Save" and name it vcpulse-weekly-brief.pdf
⚠️ Important: Make sure "Background graphics" is enabled in the print dialog to preserve all colors and styling.
