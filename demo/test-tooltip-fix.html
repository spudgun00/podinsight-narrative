Tooltip Fix Test

Steps to test:

  1. Open the main demo at demo.html
  2. Click on a data point in the Narrative Pulse chart to show tooltip
  3. Check if quotes have cursor:pointer style
  4. Click on a quote to open brief panel
  5. Close the brief panel
  6. Click the same data point again
  7. Check if quotes still have cursor:pointer style and are clickable
